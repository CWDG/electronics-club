Welcome the Electronics Club at The Ohio State University! Our organization is dedicated to fostering interest in the field of electronics in addition to educating students. We aim to get students excited about building their own projects and attending our various student-led workshops! The club has its own workspace equipped with oscilloscopes, signal generators as well as rudimentary tools, and components accessible to members. We also allot members storage space for their personal projects. We routinely offer open lab hours in which students can come in and work on their projects, ask questions or merely hangout! The club meets every other week, to candidly discuss numerous technologies and standards. Prior meetings have shed light on Serial Protocols, PWM, Arduinos, and more. We urge you to come stop by, our meetings provide excellent learning oppertunities for the novice as well the expert.

Membership

Electronics Club is easy to join, not to mention free. Subscribe to our mailing list and attend our next meeting. If 24hr lab access is desired, members must pay a nominal fee of $10 due at the beginning of each semester. The fee goes toward club purchases and maintaining the safe operation of lab equipment.

Classes

Soon, the Electronics Club will actually be offering classes related to electronics hobbies. We intend to have a soldering class at some point, as well as an introduction to arduino class.

Location

It has proven quite difficult to find our lab for the first time. To find the room, enter through the main doors on Neil Avenue (kitty corner to the faculty parking garage). Walk through the double doors and preceed down the hallway until you reach the staircase on your right. Take the stairs down to the basement landing. Turn 180° and make your way through the dingy dungeon-esque single door. Continue straight, through the large doors which may or may not be open. You are now in the Power Lab. Continue straight, up the immediate mini-set of stairs. stairs immediately in front of you. We are the second door from the left, (not the big red door) room 078 .
